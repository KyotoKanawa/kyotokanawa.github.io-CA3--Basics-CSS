Greek Yogurt Parfait

Recipe provided by Deliciously Fit, found on https://www.deliciouslyfit.com/

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1/2 cup granola
  • 1/2 cup mixed berries (strawberries, blueberries, raspberries)
  • 1 tbs honey

Equipment

  • Bowl
  • Spoon

Directions

  1. In a bowl, layer half of the Greek yogurt.
  2. Add a layer of granola and mixed berries.
  3. Drizzle with honey.
  4. Repeat the layers with the remaining ingredients.
  5. Serve immediately and enjoy!
